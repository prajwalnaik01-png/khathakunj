About KHATHAKUNJ

KHATHAKUNJ is a digital storytelling platform created for readers who appreciate deep, meaningful, and chapter-wise stories.

The platform focuses on original narratives presented in episodes, allowing stories to unfold naturally over time. KHATHAKUNJ is not just a reading site, but a creative space where stories are preserved and shared with care.

All content published on KHATHAKUNJ is original and protected under copyright law. Unauthorized reproduction, copying, or distribution of any content without permission is strictly prohibited.

KHATHAKUNJ is independently developed and managed with the aim of promoting authentic storytelling in a clean, distraction-free reading environment.
